Underground Water Tank Cleaning

At Ugoo Cleaning Solutions, we provide specialized underground water tank cleaning services. Underground tanks, unlike overhead tanks, are not visible for daily inspection, which makes them even more prone to hidden contamination. They often accumulate large amounts of mud, sludge, algae, moss, and harmful bacteria because they stay dark, damp, and poorly ventilated. If left unchecked, these tanks can become breeding grounds for mosquitoes and harmful microorganisms, causing severe health risks.

Why Underground Tank Cleaning Matters?

Underground tanks are critical for water storage in homes, apartments, industries, and commercial buildings. Since these tanks are often located below ground level, they are vulnerable to:

  • Soil seepage leading to mud and sediment buildup
  • Algae and moss growth due to dampness
  • Stagnant water that attracts mosquitoes
  • Leakage risks due to unnoticed cracks
  • Accumulation of debris, plastics, and foreign matter

Step-by-Step Cleaning Process (A to Z)

  1. Pre-inspection: Checking tank structure, water quality, and contamination level.
  2. Pumping Out: Removing all water using high-capacity pumps.
  3. Sludge Removal: Removing heavy sludge deposits from the bottom.
  4. Scrubbing: Manual scrubbing of walls and floor with safe brushes.
  5. High-Pressure Jet Cleaning: Powerful water jet to dislodge stubborn dirt.
  6. Vacuum Extraction: Extracting leftover debris and sludge.
  7. Anti-Bacterial Treatment: Using certified, eco-friendly disinfectants.
  8. Chlorination: Special treatment to kill germs and viruses.
  9. Final Rinse: Rinsing with fresh water to remove chemical traces.
  10. Drying & Inspection: Checking for cracks, leaks, and cleanliness.
